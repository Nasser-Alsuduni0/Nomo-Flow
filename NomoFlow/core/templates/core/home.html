FAQ for Salla Merchants

Will Nomo Flow slow my Salla store?

We load lightweight scripts and defer non-critical assets. You can enable/disable widgets per page.

Can I disable widgets on specific pages?

Yes, you can target by URL patterns or product collections.

How do ads connect to sales?

We track clicks and attribute conversions using Salla order data and UTM parameters.

What permissions do you request?

Store info and basic order/visitor data required to render widgets and analytics. You can revoke access anytime.
