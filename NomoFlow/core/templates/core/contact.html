Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install Nomo Flow on my Salla store?

Installation is simple! Just click the "Install on Salla" button, authorize the app, and you're ready to go. The entire process takes less than a minute.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a free plan that lets you explore basic features. You can upgrade anytime to access premium features and advanced analytics.

How do I get support if I have issues?

Our support team is available via email and live chat. We typically respond within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent issues, use our live chat feature.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard.

Do you offer custom solutions for larger stores?

Yes! For enterprise clients, we offer custom integrations, dedicated support, and tailored solutions. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
